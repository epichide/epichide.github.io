reverberation events

a brief stimulus given to one glutamatergic neuron could reliably elicit persistent network activity lasting for several seconds. the strings of regular and periodic spikes from first spike to ending of spiking is called reverberation event .

Generally, a typical reverberation episode consists of multiple bursts of network activity that are interleaved by short silent periods, as we named “reverberation events”.
