PSC: postsynaptic currents

Because current is being recorded here, the experimenter must be in voltage clamp.

EPSC

MEPSCs and sEPSCs are both measures that examine the influence of excitatory neurotransmitters on a neuron. Both mEPSCs and sEPSCs are measured in a whole cell recording configuration. The major difference is that in recording mEPSCs, the spontaneous activity of the network is eliminated due to the voltage-gated sodium channel inhibitor tetrodotoxin (TTX).

mEPSC: mini excitatory postsynaptic currents

recorded in bath applied tetrodotoxin

mEPSC 的一些参数范围

  • Simkus, C. R. L., and Stricker, C. (2002a).

    layer II neurones of rat barrel cortex:

    rise_time: 0.2-0.3 ms(fast), 0.6-0.8ms(average)

  • (Zhang et al. 2005)

    CA1 region of the hippocampal slice

    rise_time: 3-5ms, dacay_time: 7-15ms

sEPSC: spontaneous excitatory post synaptic currents

done in bath applied GABAA inhibitor.

关于 current 的拟合

简单的双指数最小二乘法拟合(Tia et al. 1996)

\[{I(t)=I_{0}+I_{f} \operatorname{cxp}\left(-t / \tau_{f}\right)+I_{s} \operatorname{cxp}\left(-t / \tau_{s}\right)}\]

  1. If , Is : the amplitudes of the fast and slow decay components

  2. τf , τs : their respective decay time constants. -> τfast , τslow

  3. if baseline current not zero can add I0 as constant.

  4. when t<0, fix I(t)=0

  5. slow component:

    \({I_{f} \operatorname{cxp}\left(-t / \tau_{f}\right)}\)

  6. fast component:

    \({I_{s} \operatorname{cxp}\left(-t / \tau_{s}\right)}\)

GABA fit

注:% fast = fast component(tpeak)/slow component(tpeak)

IPSC: inhibitory postsynaptic current

Inhibitory events can also be measured in this same way. The release of GABA is called an IPSC. To measure sIPSCs, pharmacological inhibition of excitatory neurotransmitters must be performed, possible with DNQX or CNQX for blockade of AMPA type receptors, and APV for blockade of NMDA type receptors.

psp: postsynaptic potentials

Deviations in membrane potential can also be measured in this way. The recording must be performed in a current clamp configuration for membrane potential to be measured.

关于递质释放和通道打开的 model

  1. X: calcium binding protein , X* binding calcium

  2. Ve: transmitter-containing vesicles, Ve*: binding X*

  3. nT: n molecules of transmitter T

  4. k: various rate constants

    1. 速率常数的计算:

      某一方向的反应速率只和底物浓度,催化剂,温度有关,和反方向的浓度无关.(或者说净速率是二者带符号相加)

      假设基元反应为： \[{a A+\beta D=g G+h H}\] 其数学表达式为: \[{v=k c^{\alpha}(A) c^{\beta}(D)}\]

      上式中的 k 称为反应速率常数又称速率常数 k 或 λ 是化学反应速率的量化表示方式，其物理意义使其数值相当于参加反应的物质都处于单位浓度(1 mol·L-1)时的反应速率，故又称为反应的比速率（specific reaction rate）。

    2. 其中 k3是单向的,因为它表示囊泡的释放.

  5. 在 maximal concentration of calcium-binding proteins of \(0.001 \mathrm{mM},\)情况下(Destexhe et al., 1994c)

    1. Kc: the rate constant for clearance of T
    2. \({k_{b}=10^{5} s^{-1} m M^{-4}}\)
    3. \({k_{u}=100 s^{-1}}\),
    4. \({k_{1}=10^{6} s^{-1} m M^{-1}}\),
    5. \({k_{2}=100 s^{-1}}\)
    6. \({k_{3}=4000 s^{-1}}\)
    7. \({V_{e}=0.01 \mathrm{mM}}\)
    8. \({k_{c}=10^{4} \mathrm{s}^{-1}}\)
    9. the number of transmitter molecules per vesicle was \(n=10000\)

电生理测量的一些指标

(Tia et al. 1996)

  • half width: 通常是幅值 50% 两点之间的距离
  • peak amplitude: at the absolute maximum of the currcnIs.
  • peak time: 最高/最低时间点
  • rise time: 通常是上升过程中幅值 10%-90% 点之间的距离.taking into account the noise of the baseline and noise around the peak, rise times were measured ils the time elapsed from IO to 90% of the peak amplitude of the rcsponsc.
  • falling/dacay time: 通常是下降过程中幅值 10%-90% 点之间的距离
